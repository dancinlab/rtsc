## The two walls

- **Wall A (lumen)** — no stable light source at short wavelengths nature does not
  supply (EUV 13.5 nm → 6.5 nm BEUV → shorter).
- **Wall B (rtsc)** — Fubini-Study two-lever wall: large quantum geometry (∫tr g) AND
  strong coupling (Ω) cannot coexist in one material (see H_001).

## Four meta-principles (the brainstorm converged here)

- **M1 — SPLIT**: break a forbidden simultaneity across time (Floquet), space (bilayer),
  or subsystem (outsource).
- **M2 — FRAME**: don't satisfy the constraint — change the rest frame, dimensionality,
  effective temperature, or definition of "the measurement".
- **M3 — BORROW**: draw quanta/energy from a faster or denser reservoir — nuclear,
  vacuum, entanglement, gain medium.
- **M4 — OBSTACLE-AS-RESOURCE**: reinterpret the loss channel (instability, soft mode,
  defect) as the carrier.
